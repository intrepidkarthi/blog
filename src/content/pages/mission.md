A working list, not a bucket list.

Some are big and slow. Some are small and weird. Some are already done — they stay on the list because the list isn't about ticking boxes, it's about keeping the list's *shape* honest. New entries get added every year. Old entries get retired only when they're either done or no longer true.

The legend:

- **DONE** — happened, with a year if I remember it.
- **IN PROGRESS** — I'm actively chipping at it.
- **PARTIAL** — happened, but not in the form I want it to count.
- **DEFERRED** — was on the list, no longer feels urgent. Kept here as a record of the change of mind.
- **TODO** — queued, no specific timeline.

Updated 2026-04. The illustrations are mine — drawn in code, not pixels.
