We have started TecUthsav by conducting the first contest for it. Yes, we have successfully conducted iCCC(internet C Coding Carnival) regarding TecUthsav coming on Feb9th. It is an online programming contest. We have got more number of participation than our expectation. It started at 6 PM on 24th evening(Saturday). We were in lab on that day. Night lab for us. Me, Aswin, Naagu, Jeeva and with all my junior buddies were there in our unix lab on that day. We have developed our own set of problems. All are created by us. None of them taken from the internet. Due to more requests we increased the time span of the contest from four hours to five hours. All the evaluation parts are automated. Finally we have selected three winners out of it. First and third prize goes to GCT, Coimbatore and Second prize goes to IIITM, Gwalior. We are very proud of this much reachbility of this event. I got a request from Nigeria too. But we cant allow this since it is a national symposium :) . Tomorrow my first internal starts. All of my buddies came here and we studied together. Tomorrow we have two papers, wireless networks and OS design papers. So I am busy now.
