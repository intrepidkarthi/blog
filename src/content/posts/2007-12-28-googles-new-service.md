Great new service from Google – [GOOG-411](http://www.google.com/goog411/).Make the call, tell them what city and business, get a list of business. The system can connect you to the business or send you a text message with the phone number and the URL to show you where the business is via Google Maps.

Google’s new 411 service is free, fast and easy to use. Give it a try now and see how simple it is to find and connect with local businesses for free.
