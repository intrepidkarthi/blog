We have started to build a new home for us in Old kuyavar paayam road, nearer to my school. It is exactly opposite to the mariyamman temple there in the main road.We have started the work few months back. Now it is almost completed. Tomorrow there is work going to be done for concrete work for the second floor ceiling. I think it will be completed with in next year march or april. We will shift our home to our new home then. That home is some what spacious than this. Especially i have got a very big room for me in the second floor. It is coming as my wish and my plan. There is a lot of work to do still. Carpenter and electrician work will be more hereafter i think. Builders work will finish soon i think.
