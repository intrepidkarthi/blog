I am just in shock over hearing about the assassination of _**Benazir Bhutto**_. I was away from my computer for several hours, and saw it shortly after sitting down. I ran to the TV and have been glued to it for over an hour. Personally, I always liked her, and I had read her biography (or it might have been an autobiography, but I don’t recall) sometimes back . She could have been London itself.
